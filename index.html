UGALI, na NYAMA CHOMA

Ingredients:

  • 200g of Maize Flour
  • 1kg of beef
  • Diced onions
  • Grated onions
  • Cooked Sukuma Wiki
  • 1kg of boilt beef

Ugali:

  1. Boil a litre of water
  2. Place the boilt water into a sufuria
  3. Add the flour into the boilt water
  4. Stir the mixture till it sticks together
  5. Turn of the heat
    6.

Nyama choma:

  1. Put 4 table spoons of oil into a heated pan
  2. After the oil heats up add the diced onions
  3. When the onions turn golden brown add the garlic for 20 seconds
  4. Add the meat and fry till ready

Serve and enjoy
